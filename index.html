The Process

What to expect in the commission process

STEP 1

Simply send us your photo(or an album) via facebook messages. Our artist will confirm the details and give you a time-estimate(3 - 6 weeks). If you're not sure which photo to use, check out our how to guide for pick the best photo for art commission.

STEP 2

We will turn around a pencil sketch as a start. This is the best time to give feedback on composition or make requests. Please don't hesitate!

STEP 3

Next, A colored version! At this stage, we can still make small tweaks. If you are happy with the result, you can choose to receive the painting as a print or as original. what's the difference
